Sara Duterte Responds to Growing Speculation Over 2028 Presidential Race

By Mikmik | February 24, 2026 | 4:30 PM
Vice President Sara Duterte speaks during a public engagement amid 2028 election discussions.

Vice President Sara Duterte addressed renewed public discussion surrounding a possible presidential bid in 2028, stating that her current focus remains on fulfilling her responsibilities in office.

Supporters have openly encouraged her to consider running, while political observers say it is still early in the electoral cycle. Duterte emphasized that any decision would be made after careful reflection and consultation.

Analysts note that conversations about the 2028 race are expected to intensify as political alignments begin forming in the coming years.
